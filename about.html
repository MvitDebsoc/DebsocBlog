DEBSOC is the official Debating Society of Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology, Bangalore.

The Soc focuses on Parliamentary Debating style : Asian Parliamentary and British Parliamentary debate. Sessions are held twice a week. Apart from regular sessions we conduct informal brainstorming sessions too.
