Debsoc, the official Debate Society of Sir MVIT was started 8 years ago for expressive minds to come together and engage in healthy debates.

The society works hard to achieve the most in all areas of Speech & Debate and acts as a platform for students to voice their thoughts, shape opinions and engage in healthy conversation while helping those who want to express themselves more, do so.

Activities:

We conduct Asian parliamentary and British Parliamentary debate sessions 2 times a week, after college hours to improve our skills and become better debaters.

We also participate in various National and international level parliamentary debate tournaments.

Achievements:

  • Novice Finalists at NLS
  • Winners at Soundarya Debate India Open
  • Best speaker at SDIO
  • Contingent break at GLC Mumbai 2016
  • Novice Break at Ramaiah IV 2016
  • Contention BITS Goa 2016 Open Break
  • Adj Break at GLC Mumbai 2018
  • Adj Break at BITS Goa 2018
  • Adj Breaks at Stella Maris Chennai 2019
  • Contention break at ProVerb, Manipal School of Communication.
  • Held the inaugural edition of SMVIT PD in 2019.
  • Collaborated with various clubs at college like Abhikalpana and Inferno.
  • Participates at World's Womxn Debate,2020
  • Successfully transitioned to online mode of debate , adpating to the pandemic and continue to hold sessions on a regular basis on the Debsoc Discord server.

The debsoc is proud of all of its members, who put in time and effort to be the best at what they do and hope that the energy stays the same in the coming years.
